Three side seal Pouch

We provide 3-side seal pouches for a wide range of markets with materials designed specific to your product’s needs. 3-side seal pouches are commonly used for single serve applications ranging from nutritional supplements to instant mix.

Vacuum / Frozen Food Pouch

The Vacuum/Frozen pack gives extended shelf life. It has extremely high gas and moisture barrier properties. Having Excellent mechanical properties at low thickness, great cost efficiency. Vacuum pouch is very good resistance to puncture and tearing. Wide range of oxygen transmission rates, for an increased the self-life. It provides Excellent aroma retention power. While freezing, most frozen food expands upto 9% depending on their moisture content. Thus, the packaging material should be strong and flexible to accommodate these changes. This Pouch can withstand freezing temperatures between zero degrees to -40 degrees.
At New Digi Pack Solutions, we provide high strength laminate solutions, able to withstand freezing temperature and sustained sealing. This ensures product quality, safety and shelf-life stability.

Paper Pouch

This bag usually composed of plastic and paper. The characteristics of paper poly bag are high strength, good water resistance and beautiful appearance. It is one of the most popular packaging materials which provide properties of two barrier materials at cheap rate.
Paper-poly, also known as paper laminated or paper woven, are combination bags constructed with a paper shell laminated to polypropylene inner-lining. Though the bags have a similar appearance and texture to standard multiwall poly bags, they offer extra strength, durability, protection from punctures or tears and higher moisture resistance.
Graphics can be printed in numerous colors on Paper Pouch.

Industry Uses for Paper Pouch

  • Packaging of powders or granular solid materials
  • Packaging of seeds/ Fertilizers
  • Packaging Rice/ Sugar
  • Packaging of Flours
